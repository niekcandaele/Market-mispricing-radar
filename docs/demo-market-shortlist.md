## Fast selection rule if the live ranking changes

If the live bundle has drifted by recording time, prefer a market that:
- is near the top of the current Radar
- has a readable headline reason in plain English
- does not require too much niche context
- has a clean Market Detail view with visible score drivers and caveats
- supports the product story better than shock value does
